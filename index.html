System Features

Live GPS Tracking

Automatically shares your precise live location with pre-set emergency contacts and the nearest police station.

Instant Alerts

Sends immediate SMS and App notifications to your family with a direct link to your coordinates.

Audio Recording

Once SOS is triggered, the system automatically starts recording 30 seconds of ambient audio as evidence.

Safe Zone Finder

Uses AI to navigate you to the nearest "Safe Zones" like hospitals, 24/7 pharmacies, or police kiosks.

Discreet Mode

Allows you to trigger alerts via volume button patterns even when the phone screen is locked.

Incident Logs

Maintains a secure, encrypted history of all alerts and location paths for legal and safety reporting.
